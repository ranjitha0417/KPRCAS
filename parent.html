Novels, with their expansive narratives and complex characters, offer a captivating journey into different worlds and perspectives. From the timeless tales of Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen, exploring the societal constraints of 19th-century England, to the intricate plotting and philosophical depths of Crime and Punishment by Fyodor Dostoevsky, novels provide a rich tapestry of human experience. The power of a novel lies not only in its ability to entertain but also to challenge our understanding of ourselves and the world around us.
